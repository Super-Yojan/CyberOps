Cyber Secutiry Information

What?

Cybersecurity is the protection of computer systems and networks, of all their data and information, from hackers or otehr users that may have malicious intent.

Why?

There are billions of devices that are connected to the internet and the number is only growing. We need to ensure that there devices are properly protected and kept out of reach from hackers.

How?

Protection your personal systems simply comes down to proper internet saftey. You can keep yourself and your system protected by:

  • Not clicking unknown links
  • Not downloading unknown files
  • Avoiding suspicious websites
